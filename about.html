About ISSA

Mission • Community • Student Success

Our Mission

ISSA empowers students by offering academic tools, career preparation and community support. We help students build skills, explore career paths, and connect with employers and mentors.

Community & Services

ISSA organizes workshops, peer mentoring, career fairs, and maintains resource lists for textbooks, certifications and job postings. We aim for inclusive student success and real-world experience.

The ISSA Student Hub provides a wide range of services designed to support students in their academic and career journeys. Our mission is to empower students by offering guidance on majors and career pathways, helping them make informed decisions about their education and professional goals. ISSA organizes internships and partners with local employers to provide real-world experience, networking opportunities, and potential job placements. We also host skill-building workshops, including resume writing, interview preparation, and technical bootcamps, to equip students with the tools they need to succeed in the workforce. Career fairs and alumni meet-ups offer additional opportunities to connect with industry professionals and explore employment options. Beyond career-focused services, ISSA maintains resources for textbooks, certifications, and online learning platforms, ensuring students have access to the materials they need to enhance their skills and knowledge. Our community-oriented approach also includes mentoring programs, peer support, and access to advisors who can guide students through academic challenges. Overall, ISSA serves as a comprehensive hub where students can explore opportunities, gain practical experience, and receive the guidance and resources necessary to achieve academic success and prepare for future careers.
